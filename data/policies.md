# Bright & Co. — Customer Service Policies (knowledge source)

The agent may only make claims that are grounded in this document or in the order record it
retrieves. If an answer is not covered here, it must not be invented — route to a human.

## Shipping
- Standard shipping is 5–7 business days after an order leaves "processing".
- Orders ship from our Ohio warehouse via UPS, USPS, or FedEx.
- Tracking is emailed automatically when an order moves to "shipped".
- We do not guarantee delivery dates; "expected_delivery" is an estimate.

## Returns & Exchanges
- Items may be returned within **30 days of delivery** for a full refund.
- Items must be unworn/unused with tags attached.
- Free return shipping for exchanges; a flat $5 fee applies to refunds.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days of us
  receiving the item.
- Final-sale items (anything in the Clearance category) cannot be returned.

## Refunds requiring human approval (the agent must NOT promise these on its own)
- Any refund where no order can be matched to the customer.
- Refund requests outside the 30-day window (exceptions are case-by-case).
- "Item never arrived" claims marked delivered by the carrier (possible porch theft / fraud).
- Any request involving an order total over $150.

## Damaged / wrong item
- Customer should reply with a photo. We send a free replacement once confirmed.
  (Confirmation is a human step in this version.)

## Tone
- Warm, concise, no corporate filler. Use the customer's first name. One clear next step.
